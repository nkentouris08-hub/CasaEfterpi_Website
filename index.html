About

Welcome to Casa Efterpi

Your Home on Samos

On the island of Samos, where the Aegean meets a nineteenth-century mansion, Casa Efterpi offers quiet elegance to travellers who have stopped chasing more — and started noticing everything.

Set above the harbour town, the house has watched ships come and go since the 1870s. Our concept is simple: a home run by the family who has always lived in it, open just enough to share it.

Casa Efterpi was never meant to become a hotel in the usual sense. It is our family's home, kept exactly as it was built, and shared — quietly — with those who know how to appreciate a house that has done nothing but stand still and grow more beautiful.

— The Family Behind Casa Efterpi

Our Story

Generations of Care

Casa Efterpi has belonged to our family since the house was first built — and we have never thought of ourselves as anything other than its caretakers. Every cornice, shutter, and stone step has been maintained, not modernised, by the people who grew up beneath this roof.

We open our home to a small number of guests at a time, because that is the only way we know how to do this well. There is no front desk, no uniform, no script — only a family who happens to also welcome travellers, the way Samos has always welcomed them.

— The family behind Casa Efterpi
